### Hospital KPI Hierarchy from HCRIS Data

Below is a hierarchical structure of KPIs, all derived exclusively from CMS Form 2552-10 (HCRIS) worksheets. The **Level 1** metrics are the top 6 independent KPIs from our previous discussion, focusing on financial health, efficiency, and sustainability.

For each Level 1 KPI, I've identified **4 Level 2 KPIs** that are key drivers (components or influencers) of the parent, based on logical causation (e.g., cost drivers, revenue influencers, utilization factors). These are independent, with low overlap, and directly computable from HCRIS data.

For each Level 2 KPI, I've identified **2 Level 3 KPIs** that drive or break down the Level 2 metric further.

All formulas reference specific worksheets/lines/columns where possible, using the structure from CMS 2552-10. Metrics are ratios, rates, or per-unit to ensure comparability and independence. I've prioritized high-signal, actionable sub-metrics that a hospital manager would monitor.

Presentation uses nested tables for clarity: outer for Level 1, inner for Levels 2-3.
